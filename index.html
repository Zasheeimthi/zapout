Tjänster

Certifikathantering – säker, strukturerad och GDPR-kompatibel

Swish Företag Utbetaling kräver en .p12- eller .pem-certifikatfil – ett digitalt certifikat som fungerar som autentisering för att skicka betalningar.

Många företag hanterar detta certifikat på ett *osäkert* sätt, t.ex. på surfplattor, datorer eller i e-postbilagor – vilket innebär stora säkerhetsrisker.

Zapout löser detta med modern och säker certifikathantering

Share certificate storage - Zapout offers the possibility to share the .p12 file – one part is stored encrypted locally, another part on our secure servers. Access requires both parts, which protects in the event of local device loss or theft.

Encrypted storage and reconstruction in memory - Certificates are never shared with other users, are never saved in clear text and are only recreated in memory upon transaction request.

Time-controlled access and versioning - Certificates can be deactivated, updated and logged with precise version control – no risk of misuse.

GDPR and Swish compatible - Zapout follows all recommendations from Bankgirot and Swish as well as the Swedish GDPR standard. All transactions are traceable without storing personal identification numbers.
